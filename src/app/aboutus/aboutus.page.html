We’re bringing new life to the historic town of Guildford! The Stirling Arms Hotel combines a hint of glamour with everything you love about your favourite local pub. Built in 1852, The Stirling Arms has a rich history as one of the oldest pubs in Western Australia. You can read more about our story here: Stirling Arms History Book.

The Stirling Arms is your gateway to the food and wine scene of WA’s Swan Valley. A one-stop shop for all your wining, dining, and lodging needs. We’ve created a hub of tasty food, great drinks, local rewards and a whole lot of fun. The best part, it’s all at affordable prices!
